Prestige Master Series

Riding high on the success of its launch in 2015, ‘Prestige Masters Series’, the first of its kind tournament for amateur golfers in Bangalore is back with its 7th Edition. What was started as an effort to promote amateur golf in Bangalore and South India, while breeding champions for the future, quickly grew to become one of the biggest amateur tournaments of 2020.

When you hear the terminology “Membership has its privileges” this is never more true than at Master Golf Club.
